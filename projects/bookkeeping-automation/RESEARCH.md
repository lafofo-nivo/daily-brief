## Target Customer Profiles

### Profile 1: Small Marketing Agency
- **Size:** 3-8 employees
- **Invoices/month:** 80-150
- **Pain:** Owner spends 10h/week on bookkeeping
- **Current solution:** Excel + Google Drive
- **Willingness to pay:** ₪150-300/month

### Profile 2: Freelance Consultant (scaling up)
- **Size:** Solo + 1-2 contractors
- **Invoices/month:** 30-60
- **Pain:** Messy folders, accountant frustrated
- **Current solution:** Shoebox + panic
- **Willingness to pay:** ₪50-100/month

### Profile 3: Creative Studio
- **Size:** 5-15 people
- **Invoices/month:** 100-200
- **Pain:** Multiple people uploading, no organization
- **Current solution:** Shared drive chaos
- **Willingness to pay:** ₪200-400/month

## Industry Insights

### Israeli Market Specifics:
- Most small businesses use external accountants (not in-house)
- Accountants prefer Excel/CSV exports (not fancy software)
- Hebrew OCR is critical (many receipts are Hebrew-only)
- Israeli bank APIs are limited (manual CSV upload more realistic)
- VAT reporting is painful (monthly obligation)

### Pricing Psychology:
- ₪50-150 = impulse buy territory
- ₪300+ = needs approval/consideration
- Positioning: "Cheaper than 2 hours of your time"
